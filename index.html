  4. Whoever recruits a $1k winner, wins $500

Finances

Half automatically goes to an escrow account (my Mom's bank) = $31.63 per book (which is rounded down to the nearest penny).

By the time we sell 5,000 books (via Fast Bounty), my Mom will have at least $158,150.00 ($31.63 × 5k = $158,150.00) in her bank account.

We will use $40k (about a quarter of that money), withdrawn from her bank, to distribute prize money.
